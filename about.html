About Me

As a computer science student at FAST Islamabad, I've spent most of my time in front of a computer. I am deeply interested in API development and related technologies, with a strong focus on low-level programming and back-end development.

I've been coding for a while now and love to work on tech projects. I have developed my blogs and other personal projects, as well as worked as a web developer for REX MEDIA GROUP.

One of my favorite projects is a web scraper written in Python that scrapes and downloads movies from YTS.io. It's like having my own personal Netflix, but I don't have to share with anyone else!
